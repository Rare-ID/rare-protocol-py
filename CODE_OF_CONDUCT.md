# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect participants in this project to communicate professionally and respectfully. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss ideas and code, not people
- provide technical criticism with clear evidence
- assume good intent until facts show otherwise
- keep security discussions responsible and non-exploitative

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- abusive or insulting language
- publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith vulnerability disclosure

## Reporting

Please report conduct issues to `hello@rareid.cc`.
